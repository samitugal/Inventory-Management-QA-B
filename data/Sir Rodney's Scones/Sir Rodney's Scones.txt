Sir Rodney's Scones are a classic British treat, perfect for enjoying with a cup of tea. These scones are light, fluffy, and have a slightly crumbly texture. They are traditionally served with clotted cream and jam, making them a staple at afternoon tea. Sir Rodney's Scones are made with high-quality ingredients, ensuring a delicious and authentic taste. Whether enjoyed plain or with added fruits like currants or sultanas, these scones provide a delightful and comforting snack. They are ideal for sharing with friends and family during tea time.
