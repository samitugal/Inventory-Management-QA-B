Original Frankfurter grüne Soße, or green sauce, is a traditional German condiment made from a blend of fresh herbs, yogurt, and sour cream. This sauce is known for its vibrant green color and refreshing taste, making it a perfect accompaniment to boiled potatoes, eggs, and meats. Original Frankfurter grüne Soße is a staple in Frankfurt cuisine and is often enjoyed during the spring and summer months. Its fresh and herby flavor adds a burst of freshness to any dish. Enjoy the authentic taste of Germany with this classic green sauce.
