Wimmers gute Semmelknödel are traditional German bread dumplings made from stale bread, milk, eggs, and herbs. These dumplings are often served as a side dish with roasts, stews, or sausages, absorbing the rich flavors of the accompanying dishes. Wimmers gute Semmelknödel have a soft and hearty texture, making them a comforting and satisfying addition to any meal. They are a staple in Bavarian cuisine and are often enjoyed during festive occasions. Experience the authentic taste of Germany with these delicious and versatile bread dumplings.
