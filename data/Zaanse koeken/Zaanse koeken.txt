Zaanse koeken are traditional Dutch cookies known for their buttery and spiced flavor. These cookies are made with a blend of spices such as cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves, giving them a warm and aromatic taste. Zaanse koeken are often enjoyed with a cup of coffee or tea, making them a perfect treat for any time of day. Their crispy texture and delightful flavor make them a favorite in Dutch households. Whether enjoyed on their own or as part of a dessert platter, Zaanse koeken bring a taste of Dutch tradition and comfort.
