Tofu, also known as bean curd, is a popular protein-rich food made from soybeans. It is highly versatile and can be used in both savory and sweet dishes. Tofu is known for its ability to absorb flavors, making it a staple in vegetarian and vegan cuisines. It can be stir-fried, grilled, blended into smoothies, or used as a meat substitute in various recipes. Tofu is also rich in essential nutrients like calcium, iron, and magnesium. Its mild flavor and smooth texture make it a healthy and adaptable ingredient in countless dishes.
