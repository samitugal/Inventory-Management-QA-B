Pavlova is a classic dessert named after the Russian ballerina Anna Pavlova. It consists of a meringue base that is crisp on the outside and soft on the inside, topped with whipped cream and fresh fruits like strawberries, kiwi, and passionfruit. This dessert is light, airy, and has a delicate sweetness. Pavlova is a favorite at celebrations and gatherings, appreciated for its elegant presentation and delightful taste. Its combination of textures and flavors makes it a refreshing end to any meal. Perfect for special occasions, Pavlova is a show-stopping dessert.
