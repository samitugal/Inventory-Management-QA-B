Gnocchi di nonna Alice are traditional Italian potato dumplings made from a blend of potatoes, flour, and eggs. These soft and pillowy dumplings are perfect for pairing with a variety of sauces, such as marinara, pesto, and brown butter sage. Gnocchi di nonna Alice is a beloved family recipe passed down through generations, ensuring an authentic and homemade taste. Whether enjoyed as a main dish or a side, these gnocchi provide a comforting and satisfying meal. Experience the flavors of Italy with this classic and timeless dish.
