Mozzarella di Giovanni is a high-quality Italian cheese made from fresh buffalo milk. Known for its soft and creamy texture, this mozzarella is perfect for salads, pizzas, and pasta dishes. Its mild flavor and smooth consistency make it a versatile ingredient in various recipes. Mozzarella di Giovanni is often enjoyed fresh, drizzled with olive oil and sprinkled with basil. Whether used in cooking or served on its own, this mozzarella offers an authentic taste of Italian cheese-making tradition. Enjoy the rich and creamy flavor of Mozzarella di Giovanni in your culinary creations.
