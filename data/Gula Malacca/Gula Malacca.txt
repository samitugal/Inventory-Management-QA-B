Gula Malacca is a traditional Malaysian palm sugar known for its rich, caramel-like flavor. This sugar is made from the sap of coconut or palm trees and is often used in desserts and sweet dishes. Gula Malacca has a deep brown color and a distinct taste that enhances the flavor of various recipes. It is commonly used in Malaysian desserts like ondeh-ondeh, cendol, and kuih. Its natural sweetness and unique flavor make it a favorite ingredient in Southeast Asian cooking. Enjoy the authentic taste of Malaysia with Gula Malacca.
