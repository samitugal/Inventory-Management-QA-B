Perth Pasties are traditional Australian pastries filled with a savory mixture of meat, potatoes, onions, and seasonings. These handheld pies are perfect for a quick and satisfying meal or snack. Perth Pasties have a flaky, golden crust that encases the flavorful filling, making them a favorite in Australian households. They can be enjoyed hot or cold and are often served with a side of chutney or ketchup. Whether made at home or purchased from a bakery, Perth Pasties offer a taste of Australia's culinary heritage.
