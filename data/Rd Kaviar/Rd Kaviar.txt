Röd Kaviar is a type of Swedish fish roe known for its bright red color and delicate flavor. This caviar is often used as a topping for canapés, sandwiches, and salads, adding a touch of elegance and a burst of briny taste. Röd Kaviar is also enjoyed on toast or crispbread, typically garnished with a dollop of sour cream and fresh herbs. Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, it offers nutritional benefits along with its delicious taste. Experience the sophisticated and delightful flavors of Swedish cuisine with Röd Kaviar.
