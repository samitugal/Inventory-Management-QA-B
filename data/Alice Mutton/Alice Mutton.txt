Alice Mutton is a high-quality cut of meat known for its rich flavor and tenderness. It comes from older sheep, which gives it a more developed taste compared to lamb. This meat is ideal for slow-cooking methods such as braising, roasting, or stewing, allowing the flavors to fully develop and the meat to become tender. Alice Mutton is often used in traditional dishes and is appreciated for its robust taste. Whether prepared in curries, stews, or grilled, this meat offers a satisfying and hearty meal.
