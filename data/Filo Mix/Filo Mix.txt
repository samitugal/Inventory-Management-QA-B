Filo Mix is a versatile and convenient blend of filo pastry sheets used for making various dishes, from savory pies to sweet desserts. These thin and flaky pastry sheets are perfect for creating crispy layers in recipes like baklava, spanakopita, and strudel. Filo Mix is easy to use and saves time in the kitchen, allowing home cooks to achieve professional results. Its neutral flavor makes it suitable for both sweet and savory fillings, offering endless culinary possibilities. Enjoy the delicate texture and versatility of Filo Mix in your cooking.
