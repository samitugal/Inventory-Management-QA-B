Pâté chinois is a traditional French-Canadian dish similar to shepherd's pie. It consists of layers of ground beef, creamed corn, and mashed potatoes. This comforting and hearty dish is often enjoyed as a family meal and is a staple in Quebec cuisine. Pâté chinois is easy to prepare and can be customized with additional ingredients like onions, peppers, and spices. It is typically baked until the top is golden brown and the filling is bubbling. Enjoy the simple and satisfying flavors of French-Canadian home cooking with Pâté chinois.
