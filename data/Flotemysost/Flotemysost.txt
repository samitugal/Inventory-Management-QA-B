Flotemysost is a traditional Norwegian cheese made from cow's milk, known for its sweet and caramel-like flavor. This cheese has a distinctive brown color and a firm yet creamy texture. It is often enjoyed on bread or crispbread, typically served with a slice of apple or a dollop of jam. Flotemysost is also used in cooking to add a unique sweetness to sauces and gravies. Its rich taste and creamy texture make it a beloved cheese in Norway and beyond. Experience the unique and delightful flavors of Norwegian cuisine with Flotemysost.
