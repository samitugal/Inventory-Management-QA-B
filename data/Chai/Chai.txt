Chai is a quintessential beverage in Indian cuisine. This blend of black tea is infused with rich spices and aromas, including cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and black pepper. Traditionally served with milk and sugar, Chai is known for its warming and comforting properties, making it perfect for cold winter days. Additionally, it offers numerous health benefits due to its antioxidant properties. Chai is an excellent choice for breakfast or afternoon snacks. With its cultural richness and deep flavor profile, Chai has become a popular beverage worldwide, enjoyed by many for its unique taste and soothing qualities.
