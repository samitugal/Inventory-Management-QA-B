Longlife Tofu is a versatile and protein-rich food made from soybeans. This tofu is specially processed to have an extended shelf life while maintaining its firm texture and mild flavor. Longlife Tofu is perfect for a variety of dishes, including stir-fries, soups, salads, and desserts. It absorbs flavors well, making it an ideal ingredient in both savory and sweet recipes. Rich in essential nutrients like calcium and iron, Longlife Tofu is a healthy choice for vegetarians and vegans. Enjoy the nutritious and adaptable nature of Longlife Tofu in your meals.
