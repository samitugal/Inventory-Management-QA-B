Gustaf's Knäckebröd is a traditional Swedish crispbread known for its crunchy texture and wholesome flavor. Made from whole grain rye, this crispbread is rich in fiber and nutrients, making it a healthy and satisfying snack. It is often enjoyed with cheese, spreads, or as a side to soups and salads. Gustaf's Knäckebröd is also great for creating open-faced sandwiches, topped with fresh ingredients like smoked salmon, avocado, and radishes. Its long shelf life and versatility make it a pantry staple in Scandinavian households.
