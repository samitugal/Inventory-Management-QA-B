Scottish Longbreads are traditional buttery biscuits known for their rich flavor and crumbly texture. Made with simple ingredients like butter, flour, and sugar, these biscuits are perfect for enjoying with a cup of tea or coffee. Scottish Longbreads are often served as a treat during afternoon tea, offering a taste of Scottish baking tradition. Their delicate texture and delicious taste make them a favorite in Scottish households. Whether enjoyed plain or with a dollop of jam, these biscuits provide a comforting and delightful snack.
