Tunnbröd is a soft, flatbread from Sweden, traditionally made with a mixture of barley, wheat, and rye flours. This bread is versatile and can be used for sandwiches, wraps, or served alongside meals. Its soft and pliable texture makes it ideal for rolling up with various fillings, such as smoked fish, meats, and vegetables. Tunnbröd is a staple in Swedish cuisine and is often enjoyed during festive occasions. It can be served warm or at room temperature, offering a delicious and convenient option for quick meals and snacks.
