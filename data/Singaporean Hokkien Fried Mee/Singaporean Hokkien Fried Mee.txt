Singaporean Hokkien Fried Mee is a popular stir-fried noodle dish known for its rich and savory flavor. Made with a mix of egg noodles and rice noodles, it is typically stir-fried with prawns, squid, pork, and a flavorful broth. The dish is garnished with fried shallots, lime, and sambal chili, adding a burst of freshness and spice. Hokkien Fried Mee is a favorite street food in Singapore, enjoyed for its comforting taste and satisfying texture. Experience the vibrant flavors of Singapore with this delicious noodle dish.
