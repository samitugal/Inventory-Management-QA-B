Tarte au sucre, or sugar pie, is a traditional Canadian dessert made with a sweet, creamy filling of brown sugar, butter, and cream, all baked in a flaky pastry crust. This pie is known for its rich and indulgent flavor, making it a favorite at holiday gatherings and special occasions. Tarte au sucre is often enjoyed with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Its simple yet decadent ingredients make it a beloved dessert in Quebec and beyond. Experience the sweet and comforting taste of Tarte au sucre.
