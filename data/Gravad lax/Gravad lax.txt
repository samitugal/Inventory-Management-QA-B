Gravad lax is a classic Scandinavian dish made from raw salmon that is cured with a mixture of salt, sugar, and dill. This dish is known for its delicate texture and slightly sweet, savory flavor. Gravad lax is often served thinly sliced, accompanied by mustard sauce and rye bread. It is a popular choice for appetizers, buffets, and festive occasions. The curing process enhances the natural flavors of the salmon, making it a delicious and elegant dish. Experience the traditional taste of Scandinavia with Gravad lax.
