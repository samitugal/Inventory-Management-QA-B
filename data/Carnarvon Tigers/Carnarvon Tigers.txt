Carnarvon Tigers are large prawns known for their sweet, delicate flavor and firm texture. They are sourced from the pristine waters of Carnarvon, Australia. These prawns are versatile and can be grilled, boiled, or used in various seafood dishes. Their natural sweetness makes them a favorite in both simple and gourmet recipes. Rich in protein and low in fat, Carnarvon Tigers are a healthy seafood option. Whether served as a main dish or used in salads, pastas, or stir-fries, they provide a delicious and nutritious meal.
